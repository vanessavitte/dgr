Free Comparative Market Analysis

A Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) enables REALTORS to compare your home to others like it in the market place. This comparison will help REALTORS decide the fair market value of your home in the current market. Once they have decided on a price, changing market conditions may require adjustments to the price to remain competative.

A good CMA uses the most current and reliable information from the Multiple Listing Service (MLS), real estate firms, and title companies in the community. The homes included in the analysis are sampling for properties in the area that are currently for sale, under agreement or that have recently been sold. They provide REALTORS with a base like from which to begin their comparison. Other fators considered in the analysis are square footage, location, amenities, the physical condition of your property and the effects of any existing financing on your home.

To establish "fair market value" in a given market REALTORS look at

  1. The particulars of the home
  2. The location of the home
  3. The condition of the home
  4. The time frame to sell (usually 30 to 90 days).

Put simply, "fair market value" is the estimated price at which a home should sell within a reasonable amount of time. Price could vary up or down depending on the motivation of the buyer, The motivation of the seller and fluctiations in the market.
